A hero named Thalodomos used to live near the town of Paestum.
Thalodomos, the son of Neptune, had been born in the year of Athens having been founded. 
Since Neptune wanted the name of the city of Attica to be derived from him, Athena was angry.
Athena learned of his son and went to speak to Pluto.
"I beg you to send the Furies to Paestum. The city of the son of the sea must be destroyed."
"Let them come from Lake Avernus! Beware, Neptune and your son..."
